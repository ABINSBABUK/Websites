Glory to God!

Praising Jesus, our Lord and Savior

Why We Praise Jesus

There are countless reasons to praise Jesus. Here are a few:

  • His love for us: Jesus sacrificed himself on the cross to save us from our sins.
  • His grace and mercy: Jesus offers forgiveness and a chance for new beginnings.
  • His teachings: Jesus' words provide guidance and inspiration for living a meaningful life.
  • His miracles: Jesus performed miracles that demonstrated his power and compassion.

How We Praise Jesus

We can praise Jesus in many ways, such as:

  • Through prayer: Talking to God and expressing our gratitude.
  • Through worship: Singing hymns, listening to Christian music, and attending church services.
  • Through service: Helping others in need and showing God's love in action.
  • Through living a Christ-like life: Following Jesus' teachings and striving to be more like him.

Bible Verses of Praise

The Bible is filled with verses that praise Jesus. Here are a few examples:

"For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life." (John 3:16)

"The Lord is my shepherd, I lack nothing. He makes me lie down in green pastures, he leads me beside quiet waters." (Psalm 23:1-2)

"Great is the Lord and most worthy of praise; his greatness has no end." (Psalm 145:3)
